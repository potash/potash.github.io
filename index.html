I’m a research scientist at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign’s Institute for Sustainability, Energy, and Environment.

My research is in environmental monitoring, public health, and public policy. My current focus is on agriculture and soil carbon. I previously worked on water quality and childhood lead poisoning. My methodological interests include Bayesian multilevel and spatiotemporal modeling, causal inference, and experimental design.
